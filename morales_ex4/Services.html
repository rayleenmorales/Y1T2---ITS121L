SERVICES

What does ClickNPick Offer?

Engagement Shoot

Our engagement shoot service is designed to capture the unique bond and love story of each couple. We focus on creating a relaxed and fun atmosphere, allowing your true personalities to shine through in every photograph. Whether it’s a quaint café, a scenic park, or your favorite city spot, we'll help you commemorate this special time with beautiful, lasting images.

Ceremony Video

Our ceremony video service offers a cinematic capture of your most significant moments. We skillfully record the vows, the exchange of rings, and the joyous reactions, weaving them into a beautiful, emotive film. Our aim is to create a visual narrative that brings back every heartfelt moment of your ceremony whenever you watch it.


Digital Shoot

The digital shoot service provides vibrant and high-quality images of your wedding day. Utilizing the latest in digital photography technology, we capture the grandeur and the minute details of your celebration. From the candid laughter to the solemn rituals, our digital photographs are perfect keepsakes that you can share and cherish forever.

Film Shoot

Our film shoot service offers a nostalgic and timeless quality that only traditional film can provide. Emphasizing the depth, texture, and tone of film photography, we create images that are not only visually striking but also rich in character. This service is ideal for couples who appreciate the classic and enduring charm of film.

Photo Booth

Our photo booth service is all about fun and spontaneity. Equipped with a variety of props and backgrounds, it's a perfect way for guests to capture their own memories of your big day. The photo booth produces instant prints, ensuring that both you and your guests have a delightful and tangible reminder of the celebration.
